# Workflow: Sub-Agent Integration Checklist

Use after `workflows/delegate-research.md` or `workflows/delegate-implementation.md`.
Pair with `rules/delegation-and-subagents.md` and `rules/delivery-and-verification.md`.

- Confirm the delegated task matched the assigned scope.
- Confirm no off-limits files were changed.
- Confirm the reported verification actually covers the risk.
- Re-run any required project-level verification in the main thread.
- Merge findings or edits into a coherent final answer.
